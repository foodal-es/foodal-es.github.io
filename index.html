Foodal is Comunidad de Madrid funded project in which we develop new devices to detect allergens in food. Our approach allows in situ detection, thus reducing time and money costs, and allowing restaurants to be always confident about the food they provide.

Who are we

We are a group of multidisciplar teams located at Madrid (Spain).

Our technology

Foodal technology is based in the use of Viral-Nano Particles. Those are particles once belonging to virus that have been functionalized by joining them to specific antibodies.
