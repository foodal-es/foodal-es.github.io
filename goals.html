Our goals

Allergy is nowadays an important food risk with a rising prevalence. The 10% of visits to urgencies are related to food allergy attacks, mostly due to accidental contacts with food allergens in the food.

Our goal is to develop two tools that allow to handle and prevent allergy by combining Viral Nanoparticles and antibodies to detect hidden allergens in food.
