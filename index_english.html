FOODAL is a project funded by Comunidad de Madrid, that is oriented from a translational point of view, addressing the problems encountered in the management of food allergies, both at the levels of food safety and treatment.

We propose a multidisciplinary approach integrating the consortium's experience in health biotechnology, particle nanotechnology and animal models, with the aim of developing two innovative solutions. These two solutions aim to be transferred to the private sector for its exploitation in order to improve the quality of life of allergic patients.

Our goals

Detection of hidden allergens

We are working on the development of a revolutionary tool able to detect hidden allergens in food matrices. These way, accidental interactions between allergic patients and the allergens could be avoided by means of a portable and easily usable system in all food distribution points.

Development of a new formula for the treatment of food allergy

Allergy is a pathology that currently does not count on a treatment, different than the avoidance of the foods involved and the drugs used to palliate the symptoms after an allergic reaction. Under these circumstances, we propose a new immunotherapy formula aiming to cure' or at least alleviate the disease.
